Fried fish, garlic, pepper

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Coconut Milk
  • 6 oz Sour Pork, broken up
    • .
  • 2 Tbsp. Shallots, peeled and sliced thin
  • ½ each Thai red and green large chili peppers, sliced
  • 1-2 Tsp. Palm Sugar
  • 1-2 Tbsp. Tamarind pulp juice
  • 1-2 Tbsp. Fish Sauce
  • Cilantro leaves, picked for garnish

Tools

This dip is served with fresh or blanched vegetables, such as Thai eggplant, cucumber, Thai wing beans, green onion or cabbage leaves.
